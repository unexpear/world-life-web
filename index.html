A cross-section of geologic time, ringed like tree rings and labelled on the real Geologic Time Scale. The rim is the present; travel inward through the Holocene → Pleistocene (both the Quaternary Period), through the Neogene & Paleogene — the Age of Mammals (Titanoboa, Megalodon, terror birds) — to the Cretaceous dinosaurs near the core. The reserved void lets us grow older still.

Each slice is a place & ecosystem, so 🧬 Evolution draws spokes from an extinct animal out to its living relative on the rim.
